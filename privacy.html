Privacy Policy

At Maps Scraper Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle data and protect your digital footprint.

1. Local Processing

Our extension performs all data extraction and processing locally within your browser. We do not transmit the business data you scrape to our servers. Your data stays on your machine until you choose to export it.

2. No Tracking

We do not track your browsing history or collect any personal information about your usage of the maps services you visit. We believe in tools that work for you, not against your privacy.

3. Data Security

The scraped data is temporarily stored in your browser's memory and is cleared once you close the tab or export the CSV. We do not maintain any persistent database of your extracted leads.

4. Third-Party Services

While our extension interacts with map providers, it does not share your account information or any identifiable data with them beyond what is necessary for the standard web request to function.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact our privacy officer at info@mapsscraperpro.com.
